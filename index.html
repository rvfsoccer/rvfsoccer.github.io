Vision

At RVF Soccer Academy (Ruy Vaz Forward), we aspire to be a premier institution recognized for developing exceptional soccer players and coaches. Through unique coaching and playing principles, we aim to cultivate a culture where excellence and enjoyment go hand in hand. Our commitment to "fun through excellence" ensures that every player and coach experiences the joy of soccer by striving towards their highest potential, fostering both individual growth and a lifelong love for the game. We envision our academy as a beacon of quality, innovation, and passion, where the future stars of soccer are nurtured and empowered.

Principles

  • Health and Wellbeing: Prioritize the physical and mental well-being of our players, promoting healthy habits that extend beyond the game.
  • Player Development: Focus on the soccer IQ development of players, through technical/tactical skills, physical fitness, and mental resilience.
  • Coach Development: Invest in the continuous education and growth of our coaches to maintain high standards and innovative coaching techniques.
  • Fun and Enjoyment: Foster a positive and enjoyable environment where players can have fun while learning and growing through the sport.
  • Unique and Consistent Coaching: Implement uniform coaching principles and methodologies across all age groups to ensure a cohesive and structured learning environment.
  • Excellence through Effort: Encourage players to pursue excellence by emphasizing hard work, dedication, and a commitment to continuous improvement. Players will be encouraged to develop a strong bond with the ball.
  • Teamwork and Sportsmanship: Promote the values of teamwork, respect, and fair play both on and off the field.
  • Individual Attention: Provide personalized coaching and support to address the unique needs and potential of each player.
  • Community Engagement: Build strong relationships with the local community, encouraging participation, support, and a sense of belonging.
  • Innovation and Adaptability: Remain at the forefront of soccer training by incorporating the latest techniques, technologies, and trends to continually enhance our programs.

Showcase Team

    2025 Spring Showcase Team Fees

    Fee includes coaching pay, goalkeeper training, field rental, video, and referee costs. There will be 2 practices per week and at least 6 scrimmages against college programs during the Spring season.

    Base Fee - $800

    Uniform Costs - TBD

    *any additional travel expenses are not included

    What is it?

  • A high-level showcase team competing in exclusive scrimmages against top college soccer teams.
  • Scrimmages held both locally and at college campuses to maximize exposure and experience.
  • Professional training twice a week under the RVF Soccer Academy methodology, focusing on advanced skills, tactics, and match readiness.
  • A platform designed for serious players who aim to pursue college and semi-professional soccer, providing real-game experience in a competitive environment.
  • Access to individualized feedback and coaching from experienced coaches who understand the demands of college soccer.

    • Why should I join?

  • Unmatched Exposure: Compete against college teams, showcasing your talent directly in front of college coaches.
  • Advanced Development: Elevate your game with focused training designed to enhance your technical, tactical, and physical skills.
  • Expand Your Network: Build connections with college coaches and fellow elite atheletes.
  • Compete at the Next Level: Test your skills against college-level competition, pushing your limits and preparing you for the demands of higher-level soccer.
  • Professional Guidance: Receive mentorship and advice from coaches with experience in the college and semi-professional recruitment process.

*Showcase team is designed for high-school aged atheletes.

About Us

Ruy Vaz: President and Head of Soccer: Ruy is the current head coach at Cowley College (2018-present) and has been the head coach of the Sporting Wichita First Team for 2 seasons (2021 and 2023).
